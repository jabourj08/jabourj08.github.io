Hello, and welcome to my portfolio website! I am one who has been interested in computer technology starting at the age of about 14. Between playing games, messing with file systems and dropping HTML tags into my MySpace pages, I figured out that programming languages are really interesting and even a great deal of fun at times. As I went on through high school, I ended up enrolling in a couple of design classes, where I learned a few Adobe Suite programs, and an HTML class. I went on to further my knowledge at Baker College with courses that taught me more in-depth techniques to program on the web. I now consider myself a Web Developer with a robust set of skills including, but not limited to the following:
