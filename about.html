About Me

I grew up in the city of Sterling Heights, MI where I started school in the Utica school district. In the 3rd grade, my family and I moved not too far away in Sterling Heights, putting me in the Warren school district where I completed 4th through 7th grade. After the 7th grade, my family and I moved to Washington Township where I finished out grade school in Romeo (2008). From there I have moved several more times over the years, finally landing in Clinton Township where I reside today. During that time, I have obtained an Associate Degree of Applied Science with the focus in Web Development.
